Gap Assessor: signal at submission, tracked through every stage after

The same engine that profiles the application re-runs at every downstream stage: phone screen notes, test scores, interview transcripts, manager assessments. Each new data point is checked against what the candidate originally claimed. Gaps the recruiter missed at screen surface before shortlist. Text-only means it works on everything a recruiter writes down. No video required. No biometric exposure. Works on every stage Phenom's voice-dependent agent cannot reach.

Like Cloudflare, Sentriq sits upstream of your hiring stack and stays alongside it. It does not inspect each application in isolation. It draws on network-level intelligence built from behavioral patterns, tool signals, and anomaly clusters across the Sentriq network. No changes to your ATS, no recruiter workflow changes, and no PII stored.
