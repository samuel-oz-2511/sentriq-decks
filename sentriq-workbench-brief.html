The Product

The Candidate Hiring Captcha. Not a filter. A signal.

Platform-agnostic. No recruiter workflow changes. Zero automated rejections.

Layer 1
Behavioral Baseline

Captured during form fill. Invisible. No friction. Detects bots, overfitting, and synthetic submissions before they enter any system.

Layer 2
Async Challenge

Triggered only when Layer 1 flags an anomaly. A short task framed as part of the application. Detects proxy impersonation and AI-assisted responses.

Layer 3
Human Review

Sentriq analyst reviews the full signal stack. Employer decides. Zero automated rejections. EU AI Act compliant by design.

Ongoing Gap Analysis: The same engine re-runs at every downstream stage against what the candidate originally claimed. Phone screen notes, test scores, interview transcripts, manager assessments. The trust signal travels with every application.
Cross-company fraud graph: The same fraudulent fingerprint appearing at one employer is flagged on arrival at the next. ATS incumbents cannot build this. Their customer contracts prohibit cross-employer data sharing. That constraint is permanent.
