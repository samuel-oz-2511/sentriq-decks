Sentriq. Introduction for Ronit Benbasat
The problem

Hiring teams cannot tell which
applications are real anymore.

AI tools now write, submit, and optimize applications at scale. Gartner projects 25% of applicants will be AI-assisted by 2028. The problem is not that candidates use AI. The problem is that hiring teams have no way to know whether the application in front of them reflects genuine effort or an optimized output designed to pass the screen.

The product

Candidate Hiring Captcha

Sentriq sits at the moment of application submission, before any human time is spent. It captures behavioral signals during form fill, runs a lightweight qualification layer, and enriches the ATS record with a trust signal. Recruiters do not change their workflow. They see which applications are worth their time.

01. Behavioral layer

How the application was filled out. Timing, consistency, and editing patterns that cannot be replicated at scale.

02. Signal profile

Does the application hold up against the role requirements, or does the pattern suggest an optimized output rather than a genuine fit?

03. ATS enrichment

Trust signals written directly into the candidate record. No new tool for the recruiter to learn. Works with any ATS.

Platform-agnostic. Pre-submission layer, upstream of every ATS and review tool. Think of it as what Cloudflare did for web traffic, applied to hiring.
