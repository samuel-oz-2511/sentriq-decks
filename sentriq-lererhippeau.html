Layer 1
Behavioral Baseline

Behavioral metadata captured as the application is filled. Invisible. No friction. Detects fraud signals, bot activity, overfitting, skills anomalies, and synthetic submissions before they enter the ATS.

Layer 2
Async Challenge

Only triggered when Layer 1 surfaces anomalies. For flagged applications: a short task framed as part of the application. Scoring combines behavioral signals and content quality, calibrated to the claimed profile. A proxy cannot replicate both the behavioral fingerprint and the demonstrated knowledge of the person they impersonate.

Proxy impersonation AI-assisted response Profile distortion
Layer 3
Human Review

Sentriq analyst reviews the full signal stack. Employer makes the final call. Sentriq never rejects an application unilaterally. Full audit trail for regulatory defense.

Zero automated rejections. Human-in-the-loop is architecture, not a disclaimer. EU AI Act compliant by design.

Gap Assessor: signal at submission, tracked through every stage after

The same engine that profiles the application re-runs at every downstream stage: phone screen notes, test scores, interview transcripts, manager assessments. Each new data point is checked against what the candidate originally claimed. Gaps the recruiter missed at screen surface before shortlist. Text-only. No behavioral data post-submission.

Like Cloudflare, Sentriq sits upstream of your hiring stack and stays alongside it. It does not inspect each application in isolation. It draws on network-level intelligence built from behavioral patterns, tool signals, and anomaly clusters across the Sentriq network. No changes to your ATS, no recruiter workflow changes, and no PII stored.
