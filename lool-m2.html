A2  Why ATS Cannot Copy

Four permanent blockers. None of them temporary.

ATS companies are architecturally, economically, legally, and competitively locked out of this layer.

Architectural
The signal is gone by arrival

The behavioral fingerprint forms as the application is being filled. By the time the application reaches the ATS, the submission event is over. The ATS receives a document, not a session. The signal cannot be reconstructed from what arrives.

Economic
Wrong pricing model

ATS runs on per-seat SaaS. Sentriq runs on per-application infrastructure. The unit economics do not translate. Building this layer requires cannibalizing their pricing architecture.

Legal
Liability by design

ATS companies are systems of record, not gatekeepers. Adding a pre-submission filter makes them liable for who gets blocked. That is EEOC exposure and EU AI Act liability they have spent years avoiding.

Market Structure
Fragmented and commoditizing

The ATS market is fragmented. Even the largest ATS only sees its own customers: a subjective, siloed view. And the market is declining toward commodity: Deel bundles ATS free, enterprises are building in-house. ATS was built for the 2010 hiring problem. Sentriq is built for 2026.

Greenhouse Real Talent and Endorsed detect inside the ATS, after the application has already been submitted. The behavioral fingerprint is gone. The deception already succeeded. Sentriq's layer cannot be replicated from within the ATS.

A10  Network Effects / Data Moat

Data accumulated through doing the work.

Every application processed enriches the signal graph. This data cannot be bought retroactively. It can only be earned by being deployed first.

Phase 1: Single Employer
Behavioral baseline per role

Signal is calibrated within one employer's funnel. Useful. But no network effect yet. The moat has not started compounding.

Phase 2: First 10 Employers
Cross-org patterns emerge

Mass-apply behavioral fingerprints appear across companies. The same pattern submitted to HSBC, Nvidia, and Booking Holdings is now visible. No single employer could see it alone.

Phase 3: 50+ Employers
Self-reinforcing graph

A behavioral signature that fails at one employer propagates across the network. Each new employer makes the graph stronger. Each stronger graph makes the next employer easier to close.

Why it cannot be replicated

The graph belongs to Sentriq, not to any employer. ATS platforms receive applications after submission, from a single employer's funnel. They have never captured the submission event. Merging behavioral data across employers would require data they were architecturally never positioned to collect. This graph can only be built by a neutral layer, upstream of every ATS.

Lool portfolio as Phase 1 node supply

Lool's portfolio companies become the first employers in the graph. Their deployment is not just a customer relationship. It is the seed data that makes the moat real. No other fund can offer this.

A14  EU AI Act / Regulatory Tailwind

Regulation is the procurement catalyst.

August 2026 is not a warning. It is a budget event. Enterprise legal teams are already pushing HR to act. The compliance deadline creates urgency that no sales team can manufacture.

The Regulatory Landscape
EU AI Act / August 2026

High-risk AI systems in employment require transparency, human oversight, and full audit trails. Enterprise HR tools using AI for application review must comply or face up to 3% of global revenue in fines.

UK ATRS + EEOC (US)

UK Algorithmic Transparency Recording Standard requires disclosure of automated tools in hiring. US EEOC guidance on AI in employment is active enforcement. HireVue faces a live EEOC charge (March 2025).

Sentriq: Compliant by Design
Human always decides

Sentriq surfaces a signal, never a verdict. The recruiter makes the call. This satisfies EU AI Act Article 14 (human oversight) and EEOC automated decision guidance.

Application, not candidate

The unit of analysis is the submission event, not the person. No protected characteristics are assessed. This removes Sentriq from the highest-risk EU AI Act classification.

Audit trail built-in

Every signal, every flag, every recruiter decision is logged. Compliance officers get the documentation they need without changing the hiring workflow.

Gartner names identity verification as a top 2026 CHRO priority. EU AI Act enforcement makes compliant tooling a legal requirement, not a preference. The procurement conversation has already started inside enterprise legal teams.
