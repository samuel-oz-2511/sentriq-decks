A2  Why ATS Cannot Copy

Four structural blockers. Not a feature gap.

ATS companies are contractually, economically, legally, and structurally prevented from entering this layer. This is not a build decision they are postponing.

Contractual
Siloed by design

ATS data agreements are single-company. Their contracts prevent merging behavioral signals across employers. A cross-company graph is legally unavailable to them.

Economic
Wrong pricing model

ATS runs on per-seat SaaS. Sentriq runs on per-application infrastructure. The unit economics do not translate. Building this layer requires cannibalizing their pricing architecture.

Legal
Liability by design

ATS companies are systems of record, not gatekeepers. Adding a pre-submission filter makes them liable for who gets blocked. That is EEOC exposure and EU AI Act liability they have spent years avoiding.

Market Structure
Fragmented and commoditizing

The ATS market is fragmented. Even the largest ATS only sees its own customers -- a subjective, siloed view. And the market is declining toward commodity: Deel bundles ATS free, enterprises are building in-house. ATS was built for the 2010 hiring problem. Sentriq is built for 2026.

This is why Greenhouse Real Talent and Endorsed sit post-submission, not pre. Record-keepers cannot be gatekeepers. The gap is structural, not a build decision they are deferring.
