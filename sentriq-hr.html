Your hiring process is being gamed. No tool in your stack sees it happening.

Applications are the only moment in hiring where a candidate self-describes, completely unverified, before any recruiter time is spent. AI tools now fabricate this moment at scale: manufactured credentials, overfitted qualifications, bulk-applied profiles engineered to pass ATS filters. The noise cancellation benefit is real. The fraud problem underneath it is what makes this infrastructure.

Primary: Anti-fraud

Fabricated credentials

AI manufactures work histories, references, and portfolios calibrated to a specific job description. The application looks perfect because it was engineered to. No recruiter can distinguish it from a genuine one on review.

Primary: Anti-fraud

Qualification overfitting

A real person engineers their application to match a JD exactly: keywords, tone, requirement framing, without the underlying skill. It passes ATS. It passes the phone screen. It fails on day 30. HR owns that outcome.

By-product: Noise

Application at scale

Tools submit hundreds of customized applications per day at $0 marginal cost. Even legitimate-seeming volume from zero-intent applicants drains recruiter capacity at every stage.

By-product: Noise

The signal problem

Every application looks the same. Recruiters cannot distinguish a considered application from a 30-second AI submission. The result is equal time spent on unequal quality.

How Sentriq works
Layer 1

Behavioral Baseline

Every submission carries a behavioral fingerprint. Sentriq reads it at the moment of application: how the form was filled, what tools were used, timing and environment signals. Invisible to legitimate applicants.
  • AI-generated content detection
  • Tool and automation fingerprinting
  • Behavioral anomaly scoring
  • Form interaction patterns
Layer 2

Async Challenge

When Layer 1 flags an anomaly, a short async task is served as part of the application. Not a CAPTCHA. A role-calibrated task that surfaces whether the application is human-driven. Real applicants complete it without friction.
  • Triggered only on anomaly, not on every application
  • Detects proxy and AI-assisted submissions
  • No scheduler, no recruiter involvement
  • Abandonment itself is a signal
Layer 3

Human Review

When Layer 2 flags divergence, a Sentriq analyst reviews the full signal stack. The employer decides. Zero automated rejections. Every decision is documented for EU AI Act audit trail compliance.
  • Analyst reviews: employer always decides
  • Zero automated rejections on any application
  • EU AI Act Article 9 compliant by design
  • Full signal documentation available to HR and legal
