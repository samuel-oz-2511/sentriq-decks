How Sentriq restores all three
Step 1
Recruiter Productivity

Application Behavior Analysis

Sentriq instruments the application flow and reads how each submission was completed: time per field, completion patterns, engagement with role-specific questions, screening answer depth. This happens before any recruiter opens the application. Applications with unclear evidence or missing role-fit signal are identified before any recruiter time is committed.
  • Captures application behavior signals at submission
  • Analyzes role-fit evidence and screening answer quality
  • Checks consistency across resume, answers, and candidate links
  • Zero workflow change for your team
What Sentriq detects
Step 1 output
Skill depth ambiguity
Resume lists SQL. No project, responsibility, or timeline supports the depth of usage required for the role.
Resume-to-application inconsistency
Resume says 5 years of experience. Screening answer says 2. Sentriq flags both and asks the candidate to clarify.
Role-critical requirement unclear
The role requires UK right-to-work. The application does not confirm it. Sentriq requests confirmation before the recruiter spends time on the profile.
Step 2
Shortlist Quality

Candidate Clarification Step

When Sentriq detects a specific, explainable gap, it sends the candidate a short clarification step. Triggered only when a gap exists — not on every application. Most candidates never see it. Those who do can complete it in under two minutes.
  • Triggered on 5-20% of applications depending on role — the majority flow through untouched
  • Each challenge is tied to a specific detected gap: skill depth, timeline ambiguity, missing evidence, role requirement confirmation
  • Candidate clarification updates the ATS profile automatically before recruiter review
  • No recruiter involvement required
  • Completion rates among genuinely motivated candidates consistently exceed 70% — the step is short enough that abandonment is itself informative
Step 3
ATS Enrichment

ATS Enrichment + Continuous Monitoring

Every application receives a Candidate Signal Profile in your ATS before any recruiter opens the file. But Sentriq does not stop there. Every time new input arrives in the ATS across the hiring funnel, Sentriq re-analyzes it against the original application and the clarification step. Phone screen transcript. Hiring manager notes. Interview recording. Each one is checked for contradictions and gaps that were not visible at submission.
  • Signal profile in the ATS: application clarity, role-fit evidence, consistency status, gap highlights — before the recruiter opens the file
  • Suggested recruiter follow-up questions for each gap — what to ask in the phone screen, not just what was missing
  • Continuous monitoring: every new ATS input triggers re-analysis against the original application
  • Surfaces contradictions between written application and spoken interview answers
  • Updated gap summary pushed to ATS before each subsequent hiring stage
  • No automated rejections. Recruiters and hiring managers remain the decision makers.

What Sentriq never sees
  • ✗Candidate name, age, gender, ethnicity, or any protected characteristic
  • ✗Employment decisions — Sentriq flags inconsistencies, recruiters decide
  • ✗Who the candidate is — only the application ID, tracking number, and submitted content
What Sentriq actually does
  • ✓Compares what a candidate said at Stage 1 against what they said at Stage 3
  • ✓Identifies when role-critical claims lack supporting evidence — regardless of how the application was written
  • ✓Surfaces gaps to the recruiter — recruiter makes every decision from there
Sentriq is designed as a document comparison and consistency analysis layer — not an automated employment decision tool. It does not score candidates. It does not rank applicants. It does not substitute for human judgment. The hiring company is the data controller and decides what content flows to Sentriq and when. This architecture keeps Sentriq outside the regulatory perimeter that applies to scoring, ranking, and recommendation systems.
