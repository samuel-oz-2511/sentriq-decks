HR Briefing | Application Intelligence for Talent Acquisition

Recruiter productivity. Shortlist quality. Candidate signal. All three are degrading from the same source.

Applications are the only moment in hiring where candidates self-describe, completely unverified. AI tools now engineer this moment at scale: overfitted qualifications, bulk submissions that match every keyword, generic answers that pass every filter. The result compounds across every KPI your team tracks: hours lost to low-signal reviews, shortlists built without reliable evidence, and hiring managers who stop trusting what recruiters send them.

Three KPIs. One root cause.

How Sentriq restores all three
Step 1
Recruiter Productivity

Application Behavior Analysis

Sentriq instruments the application flow and reads how each submission was completed: time per field, completion patterns, engagement with role-specific questions, screening answer depth. This happens before any recruiter opens the application. Low-confidence applications are identified before any recruiter time is committed.
  • Captures application behavior signals at submission
  • Analyzes role-fit evidence and screening answer quality
  • Checks consistency across resume, answers, and candidate links
  • Zero workflow change for your team
Step 2
Shortlist Quality

Candidate Clarification Step

When Sentriq detects confidence gaps, it sends the candidate a short clarification step. Triggered only when needed, not on every application. Candidates with genuine intent complete it in under 3 minutes. The result updates the application profile in your ATS before the recruiter opens the file.
  • Triggered only when confidence gaps exist
  • Candidate clarifies missing or inconsistent signal
  • Challenge completion updates the ATS profile automatically
  • No recruiter involvement required
Step 3
Candidate Signal Confidence

ATS Enrichment

Every application in your ATS receives a Candidate Signal Profile: a confidence label, role-fit evidence level, consistency summary, and 2-5 recruiter-readable explanation bullets. Recruiters see it before they open the resume. No automated rejections. Recruiters and hiring managers remain the decision makers. A complete audit trail is available for every profile.
  • Confidence label: High / Medium / Clarification recommended / Low-signal
  • Role-fit evidence: Strong / Partial / Weak / Missing
  • Explanation bullets: why this label, what to look at first
  • Recruiter recommendation: Review first / Review normally / Clarify before review
What your team sees
Each application in your ATS arrives with a Candidate Signal Profile attached. Recruiters spend time on high-confidence candidates. Shortlists reflect actual evidence of fit. Hiring managers trust the pipeline again.
